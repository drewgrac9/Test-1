Puppy Parlor Information

Services

A full service bath includes: bath, blow dry, brush, nail trim, ear cleaning and gland expression. If deemed possible by the groomer, we will brush out mats for an additional charge.

Pricing

At The Puppy Parlor, prices vary by the size of dog. So, an Extra Small pooch (under 10 lbs) will cost $9. A Small pooch (10-30 lbs) will cost $24. Lasty, a Medium to Large pooch will cost $32.

Packages

The Puppy Parlor has 3 different packages, but the most popular one is called the "Pooch Pamper Deluxe". This package includes a Cleansing Shampoo, Blow Dry, 15 Minutes of Brushing, Nail Trim and Trimming Feet.

Facility

We pride ourselves in what we offer to our customers! Here's what you can expect at our facility: Shampoo, Conditioner, Brushes, Nail Clippers, Towels, Aprons, Forced Air Dryers, Warm Water and Waist-level Tubs

What Makes Us Different

We give you all the tools to make your dog sparkle (or at least smell great)! It allows you to have a hands-on experience with your dog and leave the mess to us. You won't have to clean the bathroom ceiling or play a game of chase around the yard, hose in hand. All you need to bring is the dog. OH, and it's dirt cheap!
